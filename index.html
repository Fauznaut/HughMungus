Hugh Mungus

The Man

Hugh is a simple man, just trying to live his life. He wants for nothing but the happiness and safety of those around him. He is known for his steel temperament in the face of adversity, and is revered for his quick wit. Although he may die, as all humans do, he will live on in out hearts forever.
